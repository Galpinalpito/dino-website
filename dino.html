Geological Timeline

Cambrian Period

541 - 485 million years ago

The "Cambrian Explosion" saw the rapid appearance of most major animal phyla in the fossil record.

Trilobites Anomalocaris

Devonian Period

419 - 359 million years ago

Known as the "Age of Fishes," this period saw the diversification of fish and the first amphibians.

Dunkleosteus Tiktaalik

Jurassic Period

201 - 145 million years ago

The age of giant sauropods and theropods, with the first birds appearing.

Brachiosaurus Allosaurus

Cretaceous Period

145 - 66 million years ago

The last period of the Mesozoic, ending with the mass extinction that wiped out non-avian dinosaurs.

T. rex Triceratops

Pleistocene Epoch

2.6 million - 11,700 years ago

The Ice Age with megafauna like mammoths, saber-toothed cats, and giant ground sloths.

Mammoth Smilodon

About Prehistoric Wonders

Prehistoric Wonders is dedicated to bringing the fascinating world of paleontology to everyone. Our mission is to educate and inspire people about Earth's prehistoric past through detailed fossil records and scientific research.

We collaborate with museums, universities, and research institutions worldwide to provide accurate and up-to-date information about prehistoric life.

Whether you're a student, educator, or simply curious about ancient life, our resources are designed to make paleontology accessible to all.
